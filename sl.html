Context Research Insights Design

SL corporation

As autonomous vehicles continue to progress, consumers expectation on cars grew beyond just driving. SL Corporation — a multinational automotive components manufacturer and a long time partner of BMW came to Daylight in search for the new car lighting trends in China. Together, we conducted an extensive research in Shanghai, China and workshopped for ideas based on the synthesized insights. As a result, we delivered several exterior and interior lighting concepts for BMW's upcoming vehicle line: Neue Klasse tailored to SL's technical capabilities.

UX Designer

To uncover the most authentic insight, the team devised an extensive research plan in Shanghai included studying Chinese EV brands, interviewing customers with unique lighting needs, attending inspirational outings such as TeamLab, and consulting experts from relevant fields.

Insights

Sample of insight

Through synthesis workshops, we presented a set of insights with the aim to inspire product direction tailor to the Chinese market for BMW:

  • The New Heritage of BMW: For years, people buy into BMW for its long heritage of driving excellence. With the definition of luxury evolved around technology advancement in China, there is a need to redefine BMW’s New Heritage in China, but instead following the Chinese overt tech trend, develop innovative lighting solutions that feel German— humble but precise.
  • Pǐn Wèi and BMW: The Chinese word Pin Wei refers to a subtle, dignified taste for the self. An elevated quality that is admired by others. A concept that is highly regarded within the Chinese region. For lighting, that means experiences that are quietly different from mainstream makes and models. The difference is in the detail; solutions are be made tasteful through subtle and elegant execution.
  • Multi-Modal Archetypes: As a brand that excels at driving experience, BMW has the unique opportunity to cater to all multi-modal (driving vs. chauffeuring) needs and to defy the common saying in Chinese "BMW is better to drive, (Mercedes) Benz is better to ride in." Lighting can serve as the key factor in facilitating these mode transitions.
  • Exclusive Connections: With car clubs being a popular phenomenon in China, BMW can strengthen bonds between BMW owners through lighting interactions that are tailored to these uniquely Chinese car owners clubs.

Design & Deliverables

Building upon the insights, we collaborated with the SL development team to come up with several concepts tailored to their technical capabilities. Through a combination of Procreate and Figma, we illustrated how these concepts operate in potential user scenarios.

LIVING LEGEND

Honouring years of iconic kidney grille of BMW. Living Legend allows users to select grille from the BMW app to applied to the front headlighter, fostering a sense of connection across different car models and spreading BMW's values and heritage.

Users can select different grille from their BMW app.

BADGE LIGHTING

An exclusive way for BMW owners to connect. Badge lighting is enables a light projection to the rear windshield of the vehicle. The feature aims to make road trips and club activities more enjoyable for users affiliated with the BMW community. It strengthens the bond between BMW brand and the owners.

Users can select different emblems to display and choose to customize to their liking.

ESCORT LIGHT

In the journey of finding and getting into the car, the Escort Light is presented to make the process easier and more pleasantly pleasing. Especially in the relatively dark and densely packed parking environments in China, Escort Light communicates can provide the emotional value, a sense of Pin Wei, to the current and future BMW owners.

TAILGATE LIGHT

Tailgate Light supports people in completing tasks and engaging in activities around the vehicle, such as camping. By utilizing the extended area around the car, modules installed along the edges of the vehicle not only illuminate the previously dark area under the bumper but also create an atmosphere conducive for camping.

SCENIC MODE

Scenic mode enriches the BMW travel experience by removing boundaries and blending the surrounding environment with the interior space of the vehicle. Capturing the hue, tones and shades of the landscape and the ever-shifting constellations, scenic mode reconnects people with the local scenes through a subtle and novel experience.
