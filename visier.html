VISIER

OVERVIEW

Visier People transforms workforce data into insights in an easy-to-digest format so that businesses can make critical decision in managing and planning development for their employees. Throughout the 8-month internship, I worked on various projects ranging from interaction, visual, and motion design as well as some work around design system.

Some of the notable features/components I helped to design for includes Context Row, Time Window, What-If, and Button Usage.

ROLE

For the majority part of the projects, I was responsible to come up with creative design iterations to address UX, usability or technical complication. I utilized basic research methods such as environmental scans, UI component audit, and usability research for insight and data collection. I created mockups and interactive prototypes across a range of fidelities. Also, I provided visual and motion specs to certain features of Visier People.
