Aron
is not a typo.

“The Empathy Symbol stands for 2 ‘sides’ reaching out to each other, and opening up to try to truly understand the other’s experiences and feelings.”

As a Product Designer and a big fan of Emotional Design, I aspire to bring happiness to the people regardless of the scale being big or small. Whether It’s reinventing a city’s living environment, reimagining people’s end-of-life experience, or even just designing a new button. To me, user experience presents endless opportunities to make the world a better place to live in.

I believe being empathic to the user and delicate to details are the keys to creating designs that best satisfy the people. That being said, I continue to learn and apply different research methods to study the users’ needs and wants. I also put a strong emphasis on execution, whether it’s ideating, sketching, wireframing, creating high fidelity mockups, or making interactive prototypes, I always stay mindful and never shy away from any feedbacks, nor refinement.

In my down time, I like to go on to a food adventure and I am also very much into hot spring!
