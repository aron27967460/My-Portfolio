Work About Resume

Hi, I'm Aron.

Vancouver-based UX designer driven by a dream to create positive impact—without unintended consequences.

Recently at Daylight Design based in San Francisco, Munich, and Seoul. I've always believed good design should transform people's life for the better and for the happier. Driven by their everyday needs, I practice human-centered design and work on my craftsmanship based on empathetic research and testing result.

UX DESIGNER — DAYLIGHT DESIGN, 2021-2024

  • Collaborated with both local and international clients such as SCCHA, LG, EarnIn, Hyundai, Ford, Kakao and SL, for in-field research, product design and business strategy development.
  • Led design effort in most assigned projects, served as rapid reinforcement for UI and prototyping output for other projects in need.
  • Conducted research such as in-home visits and/or zoom interviews with relevant participants.
  • Helped facilitating synthesis workshop and compiled actionable insights.
  • Managed project timelines, coordinated with clients and presented to stakeholders and executives.
  • Generated multiple returning businesses for Daylight.

PRODUCT DESIGN INTERN — META, 2020-2020

  • Worked closely with cross-functional partners including engineers, UX researchers, content designers, and other product designers.
  • Designed a set of custom components for Search Result on the Facebook app.

DESIGN INTERN — PEACEGEEKS, 2019-2020

  • Designed service locating and reporting experience for Arrival Advisor (later rebranded to Welcome To Canada) and allowed newcomers to better settle in Canada.
  • Conducted research to learn about skilled immigrants in finding local employment and developed early pilot of the mentorship program SNAPP.

UX DESIGN INTERN — VISIER, 2018-2019

  • Designed interactions for navigating HR data visualizations and context.
  • Defined motion specs for time-based charts.
  • Expanded Visier design system by creating "What-if" components and universal button guidelines.
