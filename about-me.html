Work About Resume

My name’s Aron, and it's not a typo!

As a UX designer and a big fan of Emotional Design*, I perceive UX as the ultimate path to achieve my personal goal. That is, to bring happiness to the people. Whether It’s reinventing a city’s living environment*, reimagining people’s end-of-life experience*, or even finding new ways to rescue the non-humans*. To me, user experience presents endless opportunities to make the world a better place to live in.

I believe being empathic to the user and delicate to details are the keys to creating designs best satisfy the people. That being said, I continue to learn and apply different research methods to study the users’ needs and wants. I also put a strong emphasis on execution, whether it’s ideating, sketching, wireframing, creating high fidelity mockups, or making interactive prototypes, I always stay mindful and never shy away from any feedbacks, nor refinement.

In my down time, I like to go on to a food adventure and I am also a big fan of hot spring!
