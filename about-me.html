Work About Resume

Hi! My name’s Aron, and it's not a typo! I am a senior UX design student. I love the idea of Emotional Design and has always been eager to make a positive change to the world. In my downtime, I like to go on to a food adventure and I am also a big fan of hot spring!

Why UX?

I want to bring happiness to the people. In my opinion, UX is the universal access towards all the different industries. For instance, it can be redesigning the interface of some health monitoring equipment, it could be making a new package for an organic coffee brand, or it could even be reimagining people’s end-of-life experience. To me, user experience presents endless opportunities to make the world a better place to live in for the people.

What Do I Value?

The people. This is also why I eventually became a dedicated fan of UX design. Whether it’s for living quality, convenience, physical health, mental wellbeing, or simply plain happiness, I want to use UX Design as a way to bring delight to the people.

The Way I Design

I believe being empathic to the user and delicate to details are the keys to creating designs that bring happiness. That being said, I continue to learn and apply different research methods to understand better the users’ needs and wants. I also put a strong emphasis on execution, whether it’s ideating, sketching, wireframing, creating high fidelity mockups, or making interactive prototypes, I always stay mindful and never shy away from any feedbacks, nor refinement.
