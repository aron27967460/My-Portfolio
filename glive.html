THE STORY

I remembered the time when my aunt and uncle opened their restaurant in the East Vancouver. They made such delicious Chinese dishes with great portion that are certainly worth the one’s money. I remembered I would just be sitting on the side enjoying my favourite dish - mapo tofu on rice while chatting with my family. It was one of the most memorable time of my life. The memory didn’t last for long. After a couple years, my aunt and uncle had to close down the restaurant partially due to the lack of profit. That was when I realize how hard it is for a small local business to sustain the long-term challenge of survival.

PROBLEM

In my opinion, despite having great products (in this case, all the tasty food!) and services, my aunt and uncle’s restaurant simply did not have enough customer awareness to maintain the profitability. In other words, there is a lack of public engagement with the customers. This could be caused by different factors such as the limited financial support, or not having enough human resource to manage public engagement, but ultimately, many small local businesses suffer from the same issue. This brought me to ask, why isn’t there a tool that can enable these businesses to effectively raise customer awareness?
